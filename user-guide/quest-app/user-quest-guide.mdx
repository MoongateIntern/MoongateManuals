---
title: "Using Our Quest App"
description: "A step-by-step guide for using our quest app"
---

<Note>In-App Browser is not supported</Note>
<Info>
  This guide is for users participating in events that feature an interactive
  quest experience.
</Info>

<Steps>

<Step title="Accessing the Event Quest Page">
  <p>
    - Find the event's quest URL at the event location (available via a
    clickable link or QR code).
  </p>
  - Access the quest page and click the **START QUESTING** button.
  <img width="300" src="/user-guide/images/quest-app/quest landing.png" />
</Step>

<Step title="Logging Into the Quest App">
  - Log in using your preferred method (Google or Email) to authenticate.
  <img width="300" src="/user-guide/images/quest-app/quest auth.png" />
</Step>

<Step title="Navigating the Home Screen">
  Once logged in, the home screen provides several key options:
  <ol>
    <li>
      <strong>Your All-Access QR Code</strong>: Use this to redeem prizes or let
      event staff award you points.
    </li>
    <li>
      <strong>Scan QR Code Button</strong>: Use this to scan quest-specific QR
      codes at the event to earn points.
    </li>
    <li>
      <strong>Total Points</strong>: View your real-time points tally, updated
      in intervals.
    </li>
    <li>
      <strong>All Quests</strong>: Browse all available quests, grouped by
      category.
    </li>
    <li>
      <strong>Prize List Button</strong>: See the list of prizes you can redeem
      with your points.
    </li>
    <li>
      <strong>History Button</strong>: Track your quest progress and prize
      redemptions.
    </li>
  </ol>
  <img width="300" src="/user-guide/images/quest-app/quest home.png" />
</Step>

<Step title="Scanning QR Codes">
  <p>
    {" "}
    - Tap the **Scan QR Code** button from the home screen to activate the scanner.{" "}
  </p>
  - Scan quest-specific QR codes at event locations to earn points.
 <img width="300" src="/user-guide/images/quest-app/quest scan.png" />
</Step>
<Step title="Complete Quest/Adding Points">
  <p>
    {" "}
    - Once the points are added to your account after scanning you will see a confirmation page.
  </p>
 
 <img width="300" src="/user-guide/images/quest-app/quest completion.png" />
</Step>

<Step title="Viewing the Prize List">
  - Check the **Prize List** to see the rewards you can redeem with your points.
  <img width="300" src="/user-guide/images/quest-app/quest prize.png" />
</Step>

<Step title="Reviewing Your History">
  - The **History** section shows a log of your activities, including completed
  quests and redeemed prizes.
  <img width="300" src="/user-guide/images/quest-app/quest history.png" />
</Step>

</Steps>
