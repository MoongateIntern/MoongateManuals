---
title: 'Managing Your Non-Custodial Wallet'
description: 'Gain custody of and manage Moongate's automatically generated wallet.'
---
<Warning>
    Moongate has no administrative control over your wallet and its contents, and as such is unable to manage your wallet on your behalf. 
    We recommend contacting [Particle Wallet support](https://discord.com/invite/FB44yXKnRK) if you require additional support.
</Warning>

**Claiming Your Non-Custodial Wallet**
<Steps>    
    <Step title="Access your non-custodial wallet">
        Sign into the Moongate Portal and navigate to the ['Profile' tab](https://app.moongate.id/profile). 
        Click the arrow under 'Wallet' and then 'Manage Assets' to be taken to your non-custodial wallet.
        <img src="/user-guide/images/managing-wallet/manage-non-custodial-wallet/manage assets button.gif"/>
    </Step>
    
</Steps>
