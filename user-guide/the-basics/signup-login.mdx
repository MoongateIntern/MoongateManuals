---
title: 'Signing Up/Logging In'
---
For best results, please ensure that you are using Moongate in a standalone internet browser such as Chrome or Safari. Moongate 
does not currently support in-app browsers.

<Tabs>
    <Tab title="Email Login">
        <Steps>
            <Step title="Open the Moongate login/account creation page">
                https://app.moongate.id
            </Step>
            <Step title="Enter your email address">
                <img src="/user-guide/images/the-basics/signup-login/email-login.gif"/>
            </Step>
            <Step title="Open one-time sign-in link">
                Check your inbox for an email containing a one-time sign-in passcode. Enter the passcode to sign in.
                <img src="/user-guide/images/the-basics/signup-login/check-email.png"/>
            </Step>
        </Steps>
    </Tab>
    <Tab title="Google Login">
        <Steps>
            <Step title="Open the Moongate login/account creation page">
                https://app.moongate.id
            </Step>
            <Step title="Select 'Google'">
                <img src="/user-guide/images/the-basics/signup-login/google-login.png"/>
            </Step>
            <Step title="Select a Google account to sign into Moongate with">
                You will be brought to the Google account selection page. Select the Google account that you wish to sign in with. If you are signed into only one Google account, you will 
                automatically be signed into Moongate.
            </Step>
        </Steps>
    </Tab>
    <Tab title="Existing Wallet">
        <Steps>
            <Step title="Open the Moongate login/account creation page">
                https://app.moongate.id
            </Step>
            <Step title="Select 'Wallet'">
                <img src="/user-guide/images/the-basics/signup-login/connect-wallet.png"/>
            </Step>
            <Step title="Select your wallet provider">
                <img src="/user-guide/images/the-basics/signup-login/select-wallet-provider.png"/>
            </Step>
            <Step title="Log into and connect your wallet to Moongate">
                Confirm that you are connecting to the desired wallet.
                <img src="/user-guide/images/the-basics/signup-login/check-wallet.png"/>
                
                Follow the subsequent login instructions from your wallet provider to connect Moongate to your wallet.
            </Step>
            <Step title="Enter your email address">
                Enter your email address on Moongate when prompted. **You will use this email address to sign into Moongate 
                in the future** (see the "Email Login" tab on this page).
                <img src="/user-guide/images/the-basics/signup-login/wallet-flow-email.png"/>
                <Tip>
                    To connect your existing wallet to your existing Moongate account, see the [Managing Your Wallet](/user-guide/managing-wallet/connecting-wallet) section.
                </Tip>
            </Step>
        </Steps>
    </Tab>
