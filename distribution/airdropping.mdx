    </Step>
    <Step title="Navigate to the Discount & Codes Tab">
        Go to the "③ Tickets" or "③ Tiers" page within the chosen event/membership page respectively, and then click on the "Discount & Codes" tab. From here you can add new time-based discounts and promo codes.
        <img src="/images/distribution/discount-promo-codes/discount and codes tab.png"/>
    </Step>

        <AccordionGroup>

                            <img src="/images/creating-campaign/membership/creating program/Add Membership Details.webp"/>
                            <img src="/images/distribution/discount-promo-codes/time based discount/add discount.png"/>
                        </Step>
                        <Step title="Select a Ticket Type/Membership Tier to Discount">
                            In the following window, select the ticket type/membership tier to apply a discount to.
                            <img src="/images/distribution/discount-promo-codes/time based discount/select tier.webp"/>
                        </Step>
                        <Step title="Set the Discounted Price">
                            <img src="/images/distribution/discount-promo-codes/time based discount/set price.webp"/>
                        </Step>
                        <Step title="Set the Discount End Date">
                            Click the calendar icon in the "End Date" field to open the date picker. Select a date for the discount period to end. The discount period will run from the current date to the selected date.
                            <img src="/images/distribution/discount-promo-codes/time based discount/set end date.png"/>
                            Click "Add" when complete. Your discount period is now in effect until the selected date.
                        </Step>
                        <Step title="(Optional) Prematurely End Discount Period">
                            <img src="/images/distribution/discount-promo-codes/time based discount/delete time based discount.gif"/>
                            The promotional period can be cut short by deleting the discount from the ticket type/membership tier. From the Discount & Codes Tab, click the action button for the target time-based discount, "Edit", and then "Delete" on the following window.
                        </Step>
                    </Steps>
