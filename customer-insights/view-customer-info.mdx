---

## Viewing Detailed Customer Information
<Steps>
    <Step title="Open the 'Contact' tab on the Moongate Merchant Portal.">
        [https://portal.moongate.id/contacts](https://portal.moongate.id/contacts)            
    </Step>
    <Step title="View target customer information">
        Click on a target customer to open their information page.
        <img src="/images/customer-insights/view-customer-info/customer detail.gif"/>
        Swap between the "Event", "Membership", and "Benefits" tabs to view the customer's ticket purchases, membership ownership,
        and claimed benefits. Click on an individual transaction to view it in detail.
        <img src="/images/customer-insights/view-customer-info/swap tabs.gif"/>
    </Step>
</Steps>


## Tracking Customer Spending

Customer receipts can be uploaded to track customer spending at your events and within your membership programs.

<Steps>
    <Step title="Open the 'Spending' tab on the target customer's information page">
        <img src="/images/customer-insights/view-customer-info/spending tab.avif"/>
    </Step>
    <Step title="Upload receipt">
        Click "Upload Receipt" and input the merchant name, amount spent, associated event/membership program, and an optional image
        of the receipt. Click "Add" when complete.
        <img src="/images/customer-insights/view-customer-info/upload receipt.gif"/>
    </Step>
</Steps>
