---
title: 'View Sales & Attendance Data'
---

<Tip>
    Viewing sales & attendance data is an identical process for tickets, memberships, and benefits.
</Tip>

<Steps>
    <Step title="Sign into the Moongate Merchant portal">
        [https://portal.moongate.id/](https://portal.moongate.id/)
    </Step>
    <Step title="Navigate to the target event, membership program, or benefit page">
    </Step>
    <Step title="View sales & attendance data in the 'Sales' tab">
        From the Sales tab on the event/membership program/benefit campaign's dashboard, all sales data (all transactions, individual transactions, purchasers, 
        sales ($), number of registrations, etc) can be viewed. Click on an individual transaction to view it in detail.
        <img src="/images/customer-insights/sales-attendance-data/detailed transaction.gif"/>
    </Step>
</Steps>

---
Download Sales, Attendance & Email Sent Data
---

<Steps>
    <Step title="Navigate to the 'Report' Panel">
        <img src="images/customer-insights/sales-attendance-data/report panel.gif"/>
    </Step>
    <Step title="Select Event">
        Click the dropdown menu under the 'All' label to select a specific event or choose 'All Events' to include all available events.
        <img src="images/customer-insights/sales-attendance-data/selecting event.gif"/>
    </Step>

    <Step title="Select Report Type">
        Choose the type of report you want to generate. You can select one or multiple options from Sales, Attendance, and Email Sent by checking the corresponding boxes.
        <img src="images/customer-insights/sales-attendance-data/selecting report type.gif"/>
    </Step>

    <Step title="Download the Report">
        Once you have selected the desired event and report type(s), click the 'Download Selected' button to download the reports in CSV format for data analytics.
        <img src="images/customer-insights/sales-attendance-data/download reports.gif"/>
    </Step>
</Steps>
