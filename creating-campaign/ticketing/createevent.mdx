---
title: 'Creating an Event'
---

Before you can create and issue tickets, you must first create a corresponding event. 

<Info>Event creators are limited to creating 2 events. If you'd like to create more than 2 events, please contact us via [email.](mailto:support@moongate.id)</Info>

<Steps>  
  <Step title="Register your event">
    Sign into the [Moongate Merchant Portal](https://portal.moongate.id/), open the ['Event' tab](https://portal.moongate.id/events), and click 'Create'.
    <img src="/images/creating-campaign/ticketing/creating an event/create event flow.gif"/>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add basic information for your event">
    Enter the name of your event, starting & ending dates/times, address, etc.
    <img src="/images/creating-campaign/ticketing/creating an event/Fill Event Basic Info.png"/>
    <Warning>
        While you can edit most of this information even after your event goes live,
        your event's URL **cannot be changed after publication.** Be sure to double-check it
        before publication.
    </Warning>
    When you are ready, scroll to the end of the page and click 'Next'.
    <Tip>
        Tip: Clicking "Next" will save your work. You can also click "Save Draft" at any point
        to save your work and return to it later.  
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill out the details of your event">
    Add a featured image, event banner, and description of your event, as well as links to relevant social media accounts.
    <img src="/images/creating-campaign/ticketing/creating an event/Fill Event Details.png"/>
    <Tabs>
        <Tab title="Featured Image">
            <img src="/images/creating-campaign/ticketing/creating an event/Featured Image Location.png"/>
        </Tab>
        <Tab title="Event Banner">
            <img src="/images/creating-campaign/ticketing/creating an event/Event Banner Location.png"/>
        </Tab>
        <Tab title="Event Description">
            <img src="/images/creating-campaign/ticketing/creating an event/Event Description Location.png"/>
        </Tab>
        <Tab title="Socials/Website Links">
            <img src="/images/creating-campaign/ticketing/creating an event/Socials Location.png"/>
        </Tab>
    </Tabs>
        When you are ready, scroll to the end of the page and click 'Next'. You are now ready to [create tickets](/creating-campaign/ticketing/createtickets)!
  </Step>
</Steps>
