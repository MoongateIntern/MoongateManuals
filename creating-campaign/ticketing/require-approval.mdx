<Tip>
  Event tickets supported with an approval functionality, allows organizers to
  create tickets that can only be granted by the organizer.
</Tip>

        <Note>

            * **Maximum Purchase per user:** 1
            * **Restrictions:** Users cannot buy tickets for others
            * **Maximum Purchase per Transaction:** 1
        </Note>
