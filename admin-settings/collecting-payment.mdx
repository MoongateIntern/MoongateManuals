---
title: 'Collecting User Payments'
---

<Warning>
    If you are collecting payment for tickets or memberships, you must connect a bank account or crypto wallet to receive your payout. 
    Additionally, please contact [Moongate support](mailto:support@moongate.id) if you will be collecting payment for your event or membership program.
</Warning>

<Steps>
    <Step title="Navigate to the Payment settings panel">
        Click 'Setting' on the sidebar, and then 'Payment.'
        <img src="/images/admin-settings/collecting-payment/payment panel.gif"/>
    </Step>
    <Step title="Add a bank account/crypto wallet">
        Click 'Add Bank Account' or 'Add Crypto Wallet' according to your preference and fill out the requested information in the 
        subsequent pop-up window.
        <img src="/images/admin-settings/collecting-payment/add wallet button.png"/>
    </Step>
</Steps>
